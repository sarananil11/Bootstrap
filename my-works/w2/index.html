ROLEX

Rolex is one of the most iconic luxury watch brands in the world, renowned for its precision, craftsmanship, and timeless design. Founded in 1905 by Hans Wilsdorf and Alfred Davis in London (later moving to Geneva, Switzerland), Rolex has become a symbol of prestige and success.

G-SHOCK

G-Shock, created by Casio in 1983, is a watch brand famous for its extreme durability, rugged design, and shock resistance. Built with the idea that a watch should “never break,” the first G-Shock (DW-5000C) set the standard for toughness.
